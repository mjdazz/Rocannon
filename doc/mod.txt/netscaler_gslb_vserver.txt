SYNOPSIS                   *netscaler_gslb_vserver*

     • Configure gslb vserver entities in Netscaler.

REQUIREMENTS                   *netscaler_gslb_vserver-requirements*

   The below requirements are needed on the host that executes this module.

     • nitro python sdk
